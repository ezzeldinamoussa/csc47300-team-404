Turn your to-do list into a competition.

StreakQuest encourages you to do your daily tasks in an interactive way. Compete with friends, earn points, and build streams to make productivity enjoyable.

Core Features

  • Daily Task Lock-In: Users set tomorrow’s tasks, which lock after 11:59 PM (no deleting, only adding).
  • Points & Streaks: Each completed task earns points. Hitting 75%+ keeps your streak alive, while missing too many breaks it.
  • Friends & Competition: Add friends, see their streaks and points, and battle on leaderboards. Shared tasks with friends = double points.
  • Rewards Shop: Spend points on profile badges, skins, or “power-ups” like lifelines to skip tasks or revive broken streaks at higher cost.
  • Achievements: Milestones (7-day streak, 100 tasks completed, first shared task) earn badges and bragging rights.
